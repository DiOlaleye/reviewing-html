In this fan page, we'll be going through each current Golden State Player and taking a deeper dive into them

Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry is widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. He has revolutionized the game with his incredible shooting range and ability to make shots from virtually anywhere on the court. Curry has won multiple MVP awards and has led the Warriors to several NBA championships. He is currently heading into his 17th NBA season as he looks to lead the Warriors to another championship.

Accomplishments

  • 4 time Champion
  • 2 time MVP
  • 11 time all star

Jimmy Butler III

Butler was aquired on February 6, 2025 as part of a 5 team deal that agreed to send Butler to the Golden State Warriors. He is an excellent 2-way player and is a master of getting to the free throw line. With him and Draymond Green in the frontcourt, this Golden State defense is scary

Accomplishments

  • 6 time All Star
  • 5 time All NBA selection
  • Most Improved player (2015)

Draymond Green

Draymond Green is known for his versatility, defensive prowess, and leadership. He is regarded as one of the best defensive players of all time, and he has been crucial in helping the Warriors secure multiple championships. Green's ability to guard multiple positions and facilitate the offense makes him an invaluable asset to the team.

Accomplishments

  • 4 time Champion
  • 1 time NBA Defensive Player of the Year
  • 3 time All Star

Brandin Podziemski

Entering his 3rd NBA season, Podziemski has already established himself as a secondary playmaker beside Steph Curry. The 22 year old is a reliable shooter and playmaker, and he's a solid defender, a role which the Warriors value greatly. His prescence has allowed Steph Curry to play off ball more often, which becamea strenght of their offense in the 2024-2025 season. Overall, Podziemski is a rising star in the league and could become an All-NBA player in the near future.

Accomplishments

  • NBA All-Rookie First Team (2024)
  • 2 Rising Stars selections
